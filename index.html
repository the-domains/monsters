A Brief for the Defense, by Jack Gilbert

"Sing your own song, said the river,
"Sing your own song.

"Out of yesterday song comes.
It goes into tomorrow,
Sing your own song.

"With your life fashion beauty,
This too is the song.
Riches will pass and power. Beauty remains.
Sing your own song.

"All that is worth doing, do well, said the river.
Sing your own song.
Certain and round be the measure,
Every line be graceful and true.
Time is the mold, time the weaver, the carver,
Time and the workman together,
Sing your own song.

Sing well, said the river. Sing well."

The manifest was pushed aside
It hardly went to sprout
so life came crashing forward
with a rumble and a shout.

I’ve got this midnight feeling
from the past few heavy days
this manifest is growing
and its weight is plain inside.

So what’s this all about you say
when will it come to pass?
when words are written down I say
emotions grow like grass.
